    Alpha's

    Here is a list of all the Alpha's of Hello Neighbor.

    1. Pre-Alpha
    2. Alpha 1
    3. Alpha 1.5
    4. Alpha 2
    5. Alpha 3
    6. Alpha 4

    The Hello Neighbor Pre-Alpha was the first model people could play.
    Both the Pre-Alpha and Alpha 1 had a nighmare. In the Pre-Alpha use could use the nighmare for a glitch by getting a trash can from the neighbor's house and then put it on the players bed. In Hello Neighbor Alpha 1 gave us some more info about why we were sneaking in to the neighbor's house. Alpha 1.5 was never released so we don't know anything about it. Alpha 2 was a huge update!!! How Huge you ask? Well it eventually became Act 1 one of Hello Neighbor. Alpha 3 was another huge update because it became Act 3.
    Alpha 4 was the same as Alpha 3 except it became beta 1 and 2.
